ABOUT AV CAR DETAILING

AV Car Detailing is a Mobile Car Cleaning and Detailing service which has been in operation since 2003. From a Mini Detail to a Full Pre-Sale Detail, we cater to our clients particular needs and requests. We offer services such as Paint Protection, Machine Polishing, Full Interior Detailing, Leather Cleaning and Rejuvenation, Exterior Detailing, Engine Bay Detailing and Regular Mini Detail/Maintenance Details. We use quality products and deliver service of a high standard. We are fully insured and your vehicle will be in the best of hands when entrusted to us.
